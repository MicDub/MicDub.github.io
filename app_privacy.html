Privacy Policy

Hello! I'm Michael Dubbeldam. Your privacy matters to me, and I want to explain how the Max Speed app handles your location information.

What We Collect

The Max Speed app collects GPS location data to provide you with accurate, real-time speed and traffic information.

How We Use Your Data

All GPS location data is stored anonymously. We do not collect any personal identifiers, such as your name, email address, or IP address. This location information is used solely for dynamic traffic measurements.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Anonymous location data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties except in aggregate, anonymized form to enhance traffic analysis.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please feel free to reach out to me at info@dubbeldam.com.
